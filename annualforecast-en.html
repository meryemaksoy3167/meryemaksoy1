Analysis Methods

Two Powerful Tools, One Holistic View

The annual forecast analysis combines two complementary methods to offer you a comprehensive map of the period ahead.

☀️

Solar Return Chart

Each year when the Sun returns to your exact birth degree, a new annual chart is formed. This chart shows the theme, opportunities, and challenges of that year.

🔭

Transit Analysis

The transits of Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Pluto over the planets in your natal chart reveal in which area and during which period there is pressure or opportunity.

🌕

Progression Chart

The progressed chart, which shows how your natal chart evolves over time, reveals the periods of transformation in your inner world.

📊

Monthly Key Periods

The personal impact of lunar eclipses, solar eclipses, and significant planetary transits — identifying which months are critical for you.

💼

Career & Financial Windows

Jupiter transits and 2nd/10th house activations reveal during which periods the ground is ready for a career leap and financial growth.

❤️

Relationship & Emotional Periods

Venus and Moon progressions along with 7th house transits show during which periods significant developments in relationships will intensify.

Why Does It Matter?

Reading the Sky in Advance

Astrology is not a map of fate — it is a map of opportunities and energy. Knowing the energy of the period ahead allows you to see which topics deserve your focus and when, and which periods call for patience versus forward movement.

"You cannot change the wind; but you can adjust your sails."

When we present the solar return and transit analysis together, both the year's overall theme and the critical monthly turning points come into focus. Together we identify the most favorable periods for your most important steps.
