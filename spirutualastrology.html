Core Concepts

Past & Future: The Two Nodes

The heart of spiritual astrology beats along the axis formed by the North Node and South Node. This axis shows where your soul has come from — and where it is trying to go.

☋

South Node

Your Past Life Roots

The South Node represents the areas your soul has worked deeply in past lifetimes. This territory feels natural and comfortable — but remaining stuck here blocks your growth. The sign and house of the South Node show where you have come from, which habitual patterns you carry, and what needs to be released.

☊

North Node

Your Spiritual Purpose in This Lifetime

The North Node points to the area you are called to grow and evolve into in this lifetime. It may feel unfamiliar and challenging at first — because this is new territory for your soul. Yet this is precisely where you find the deepest fulfillment. Living in the direction of your North Node means aligning with your spiritual purpose.

Why Spiritual Astrology?

Understanding Recurring Patterns

The answer to the question "why do I keep experiencing the same problems?" is often hidden in your spiritual chart. Recurring relationship patterns, persistent obstacles in your career, or inexplicable fears may be pointing to lessons the soul has not yet completed.

"Karma is not a punishment — it is a lesson awaiting completion; a gift awaiting transformation."

Spiritual astrology illuminates these patterns without judgment, with love. When you understand why you have come together with these people, under these circumstances, in this lifetime — transformation begins. This session is the map of the transition from victim story to conscious creator.
