Analysis Method

A Three-Layer Reading

Synastry analysis is a holistic reading of three separate charts: your personal chart, your partner's chart, and the composite chart born from the union of the two.

"When two souls choose each other, the sky has already written the codes of that choice."

Many relationship analyses look only at Sun sign compatibility. Yet real harmony and tension are hidden in the deeper planetary contacts — Moon-Moon, Venus-Mars, Saturn-Moon and more. All of these layers are uncovered in this session.

The session is designed as a live, shared exploration — one where both people listen, feel, and truly understand each other. The deepest meanings of the charts emerge when both energies are present in the same space at the same time.
