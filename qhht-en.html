Foundations

The Healing Power of the Subconscious

QHHT (Quantum Healing Hypnosis Technique) is a subconscious regression therapy method developed by world-renowned author and researcher Dolores Cannon through more than 50 years of dedicated work.

This method makes it possible to access the records of the subconscious by reaching the Theta brainwave state — a deep level of hypnotic consciousness. Beyond exploring past lives, it creates the foundation for direct communication with the person's highest level of consciousness, also known as the Higher Self.

Throughout the session, the client remains in full awareness — observing, feeling, and sharing what unfolds. The therapist only guides — the healing and insight always come from within the person themselves.

🧠

Root-Level Healing

The Process

How Does a Session Work?

A typical QHHT session lasts between 4 and 6 hours and consists of four core phases.

01
🗣️

Pre-Interview

The client's life story, intention, and questions are heard. A safe, open, and comfortable space is established. Duration: 1–2 hours.

02
🌀

Hypnotic Regression

With therapist guidance, the Theta state is entered. The client journeys to past lives or scenes significant to the soul. Duration: 1–2 hours.

03
💬

Higher Self Dialogue

Communication with the Higher Self is established, all questions prepared during the pre-interview are answered, and physical and emotional healing is requested. Duration: 1–2 hours.

04
🌿

Integration & Recording

The session is delivered with an audio recording. Listening to the recording repeatedly deepens the transformation. A follow-up support message is sent within 48 hours after the session.
